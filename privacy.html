Privacy Policy

Triculator

Last updated: 7 June 2026

Triculator is built to respect your privacy completely. The app performs all calculations directly on your device and does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Information we collect

None. Triculator does not collect any personal data. It has no account system, no sign-in, and no way to identify you. Every value you enter and every result you calculate stays on your device.

How your data is used

The numbers you type into Triculator are used only to perform the calculation you requested, on your device, in that moment. They are not transmitted to us, stored on a server, or used for any other purpose.

Analytics and tracking

Triculator does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies. It does not use cookies, device identifiers, or third-party software development kits (SDKs) that would collect information about you.

Third-party services

Triculator does not share data with third parties, because it does not collect data in the first place. The app functions entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to work.

Children's privacy

Triculator does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact
